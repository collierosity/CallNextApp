About

Do you ever daydream during a meeting and think of five people you need to contact but then later when you're in your car or on the train, you end up listening to music or a podcast and completely forget? If so, this app is for you.

At the moment during the meeting when you think of the person you need to call, or when a text message or email comes in that requires a longer discussion, add that person to your queue.

  • Search the contacts on your iPhone or other device
  • Select an app for the call such as Phone or FaceTime Video, or decide later
  • Select a phone number or email address, or wait until you make the call
  • Add the call to be next or at the end of your queue, or schedule a reminder for a specific day and time

Siri

  • "Hey Siri, let's get started with Call Next"
  • "Hey Siri, who is on deck in Call Next?"
  • "Hey Siri, open the first in Call Next?"
  • "Hey Siri, add ___ to the queue in Call Next"

Additional time-savers:

  • Save frequently used contacts as favorites, with or without call details
  • Use your iPad during a meeting and your iPhone in the car, with queue kept in sync
  • Add calls your queue directly from your Contacts app using the Share button
  • Let the app auto-select your most frequently used call details
